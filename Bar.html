Aluminum Bar Shape Corner

Description

Aluminum Flat Bar is availble in various alloys offering a range of weldability, corrosion resistance and machinability. High strength aluminums can be used in aerospace applications, and high corrosion resistance aluminum alloys are commonly used in marine applications.
Aluminum Square Bar (Rod) is availble in various alloys offering a range of weldability, corrosion resistance and machinability. High strength Aluminum Square bar, such as alloy 7075, can be used in aerospace applications, and for high strength to weight rations, Aluminum Alloy 2024-T351 Square Bar is commonly used.
Aluminum Round Bar is availble in various alloys offering a range of weldability, corrosion resistance and machinability. High strength Aluminum Round Rod can be used in aerospace applications, and high corrosion resistance aluminum alloy round rods are commonly used in marine applications. Aluminum Round Rod may be cold finished or extruded.
Aluminum T-Bar is the most commonly used aluminum for structural applications. It has above average corrosion resistance, good machinability, and is excellent for welding.
