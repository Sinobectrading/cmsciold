Aluminum 7xxx - Al-Zn Alloys

Description

The 7xxx alloys are heat treatable and among the Al-Zn-Mg-Cu versions provide the highest strengths of all aluminum alloys.There are several alloys in the series that are produced especially for their high toughness,notably 7150 and 7475, both with controlled impurity level to maximize the combination of strength and fracture toughness.
  • Heat treatable
  • Very high strength;special high toughness versions
  • Aerospace,automotive applications
  • Representative alloys: 7005,7075, 7475, 7150
  • Typical ultimate tensile strength range:32-88 ksi
