Galvanized Steel

Description

Galvanization is the process of applying a protective coating to steel or iron. The coating is made from zinc and is used to halt the formation of rust. Galvanization is important because it provides long-lasting protection for steel and iron products.
  • Main frame is made of hot roll H-style steel or welded H-style.
  • Fast construction, easy installation, time saving, labor-saving and stable structural.
  • Light weight, high strong, large span and can improve higher space efficiency.
  • It can be pre-fabricated and customire design and earthquake proofing, waterproofing, anticorrosion, beautiful appearances.
  • It has a good ability to assemble at site, and free in designing partitioning and transportation.
  • It has a low foundation cost, energy conserving and environmental protection, and it can be recycled and be use circularly.
  • Usage: workshop, office, warehouse, single-layered heavy plants, large-span public buildings, multi-storey.
