Description: Aluminum Sheet (Plate) is available in various alloys offering a range of weldability, corrosion resistance and machinability. High strength Aluminum Sheet, such as 7075, can be used in aerospace applications, and high corrosion resistance aluminum alloy sheets are commonly used in marine applications.
